Privacy Policy

We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy

Information We Collect

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name, email address, preferences, and usage patterns within the app. When you sign up for updates via our website, we store your email for future communication about the app launch and related content.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to improve the user experience, personalize content, notify you about updates or promotions, and analyze app engagement. We do not sell your information to third parties.

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to track user activity on our website. This helps us optimize our design, marketing, and feature rollout strategy. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Our app and website may integrate with third-party services such as Google Analytics or Firebase. These providers may collect anonymized data subject to their own privacy policies.

Email & Notifications

If you join our email list, we may send occasional updates about our product. You can unsubscribe anytime using the link in the email or by contacting us directly.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

User Privacy

BarBuddy is intended for users 21 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 21 years of age. If you believe we have, please contact us to request deletion.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at contact@barbuddy.app.
